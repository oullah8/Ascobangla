Introduction

Dear Sir,

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as an Overseas Recruiting Agency recognized by the ministry of Labor, Government of Bangladesh. We have an excellent track record of providing appreciable service to Government organization and reputed companies in Middle East, involved in various large engineering, construction and medical projects. Our administration office is located at a strategic location in the heart of the capital of Bangladesh. We have an efficient organizational setup and satisfying service.

Arabian Service Company has been at the forefront of recruitment services, offering both employees and employers a wide range of professional services.

With a reputation to safeguard the interest of both employees as well as the employers and to provide expertise and ethical standards in placements of truly qualified workers, we continue to be committed to the highest standards in Human Resources and Placement.

Through the many years of working experience as Placement Consultants, we have grown immensely and our achievements and performances have been particularly outstanding in the Middle East and the Gulf, where our list of satisfied clients is log and impressive. And our best credentials are the thousands of men we have deployed so far and their splendid record of services on the job performance.

While working with the areas of specialization, as and when we receive the demand order from our clients we try to locate suitable candidate through our Data Bank and from our branches spread all over Bangladesh and through newspaper advertisements we collect substantial candidates. Then through our panel of technical experts we conduct through interviews evaluating the mental aptitude of the prospective candidates and their suitability for the job and the place of employment, after which we short-list the most suitable candidates.

Our Clients are the ultimate beneficiaries of our professionalism. The Quality, Time Management and the state of the art of communication systems is the main features of our organization. We arrange only the short-listed candidates for the final Interview with delegates. Our entire system of requirements and time bound deployments are widely appreciated by all of our clients. Unlike other in the field we don’t depend only on the head for of the deployments we have our own branches in most Mega cities of Bangladesh, which save recruitment time and makes it easy for us to select right people for the right job, to keep up the time schedule of the sponsor too. Our capability covers a gamut of services that could meet your needs.

It would be a pleasure to serve your good selves to the utmost satisfaction for fostering your business to sail smoothly efficiently and economically. We therefore welcome this opportunity to request you to confer on us to act and function as your representative in the above said capability and thereby gain your trust, confidence and goodwill.

Please feel free to contact us by phone, fax and e-mail of directly to our mobile number in any time for further information regarding recruitment of manpower from Bangladesh and we will be always at your service. We always regard our clients as our most important valuables.

With our agile, efficient, prompt and guaranteed service, we assure you of quality that is forever.

With warm regards,

Yours truly,
